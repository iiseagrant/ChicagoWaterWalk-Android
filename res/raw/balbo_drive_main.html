Most of the land around you is part of Grant Park. Known as "Chicago's front yard," the park was built to create public space along the lakefront and to act as a buffer between the busy downtown and the lake. In addition to flower gardens, open lawns, and trees, the park has plots of native prairie grasses and wildflowers that can absorb water and filter out pollutants. Grant Park is the site of popular food and music festivals and the home of numerous sculptures and large art installations.
